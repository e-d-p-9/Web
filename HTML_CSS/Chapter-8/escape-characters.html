Escape characters

There are some special characters reserved by html so to print them out we need to use special character combinations as a representation:


Character Name
< Less-than
> Greater-than
& Ampersand
" Quotation mark
¢ Cent sign
£ Pound sign
¥ Ye sign
€ Euro sign
© Copyright symbol
® Registered trademark
™ Trademark
‘ Left single quote
’ Right signle quote
” Left double quotes
” Right double quotes
× Multiplication sign
÷ Division sign
